Family Business Automation for Chicken Feed Company

Why This Project?

Managing a chicken feed business involves many daily tasks — handling supplier orders, dealer payments, stock management, and employee salaries. Previously, these were done manually, causing errors and delays.

What We Built

An automated spreadsheet system that simplifies and tracks all these operations in one place — easy for our team to use.

How It Works — Process Flow

1. Receive Supplier Raw Materials & Record Purchase
2. Mix and Produce Chicken Feed Stock
3. Sell Feed to Dealers and Track Partial Payments
4. Monitor Stock & Expenses (Salaries, Overheads)
5. Generate Reports for Business Insights

Key Benefits

  • Accurate tracking of payments and outstanding balances
  • Real-time stock updates to avoid shortages
  • Clear financial dashboards for quick decisions
  • User-friendly for all staff levels

Business Impact

This system saved us hours every day, reduced costly mistakes, and gave us confidence to grow the business with better control.
